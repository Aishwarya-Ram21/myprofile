SPARROWS

The term "sparrow" covers a wide range
 of relatively small, mostly drab brown birds, 
which birders often call "LBJs" or "little brown
 jobs" because they can be notoriously difficult
 to identify. While many of these birds include
 the word "sparrow" in their common names, other
 types of sparrows include buntings, towhees, and 
juncos. In fact, there are dozens of different sparrow
